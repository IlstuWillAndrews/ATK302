Carlos the Dragonfly is the main character in a 2d side scroller.
He uses his lazer eyes in order to destroy bad guys (which happen to be flies) as he manuvers around obstacles with his magnificent wings
Carlos has a special tail and legs that activate when he uses his lazer ability.
His tail glows blue and his legs extend when he fires the lazers creating an added challenge not to touch any obstacles
His tail also functions as a bomb dropper (which is a powerup available in the 2d side scroller)
